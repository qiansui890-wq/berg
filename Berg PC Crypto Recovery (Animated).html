How Recovery Works

From theft to recovery.

01

Intake & review

You tell us what happened. We review the facts, the platforms involved, and whether — and how — we can help. The initial review is free.

02

Trace the funds

Our forensics team maps the flow of stolen assets across the chain, building an admissible chain-of-funds report.

03

Identify & locate

We cluster destination wallets, attribute them to actors and exchanges, and pinpoint where the money can be reached.

04

Freeze

We coordinate with exchanges and move the courts for injunctions that freeze the criminal wallets.

05

Recover

We litigate to return your funds — including across borders. You pay nothing unless we recover.

⚠️

Beware of recovery scams

Berg PC will never ask you to pay an upfront fee to recover stolen funds, and we cannot act until a full investigation is complete. If anyone guarantees recovery for a fee, stop and contact us directly.
