One-line: The base surface — white, hairline border, restrained shadow; `interactive` adds a hover lift.

```jsx
<Card padding="lg" interactive>
  <CardHeader eyebrow="Crypto Litigation" title="Crypto Fraud & Recovery" trailing={<Badge tone="success">Active</Badge>} />
  <p>We trace, freeze, and recover stolen digital assets.</p>
</Card>
```
`padding`: none/sm/md/lg. Use `as="a"` for link cards.
